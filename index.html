Here's how you make your Chicken Dum Biryani

Ingridients

  • Chicken (1 kilkogram)
  • Basmati Rice (1 kilogram)
  • Spices
    • Cumin seeds (1-2 teaspoons)
    • Bay leaves (3-4 leaves)
    • Cardomon (6-7 pods)
    • Red chilli powder (2-3 tablespoons)
    • Turmeric powder (2 teapoons)
    • Garam Masala (1 tablespoons)
    • Cinnamon (2 sticks)
    • Cloves (6 pieces)
    • Green Chillies (6-7 pieces)
  • Onions (3 onions thinly sliced)
  • Garlic paste (1 tablespoon)
  • Ginger paste (3 tablespoons)
  • Corrianders (Hand full)
  • Mint leaves (Hand full)
  • Curd (4 tablespoons)
  • Oil as per requirement
  • Water as per requirement
  • Salt to taste

Let's make our biryani !

  1. Cut the chicken pieces into edible pices and wash it thoroughly.

  2. For marination

    In a bowl add in your chicken with curd, 1 tablespoon chilli powder, add in greeen chillies with a cut along the lengths, garam masala, and salt to taste.
    Mix well and let it marinate for 30 mins.



  3. Cooking the basmati Rice

    Wash the rice thoroughly and keep it aside.
    In a kadai add 1 table spoon oil, add add a bay leaf, 3 pieces of cloves, 1 cinnamon stick, 3 cardomon pods, fry them all together for 2 mins on midium flame.
    Add 10 to 12 glasses of water and salt as per requirement along with the basmati rice. Cook the rice until its almost cooked (90% cooked rice), keep it aside.



  4. Preparing the Biryani gravy

    5. In a kadai add 3-4 tablespoons of oil. After the oil is heated add the cumin seeds, 2 bay leaves, 3 cardomon pods, 1 cinnamon stick, 3 clove pieces and the thinly sliced onions. Fry them alll together until the onion turns dark brown in colour. Keep aside half of the fried onions.
    Add the ginger and garlic pastes, 1 tablespoon chilli powder, 2 teaspoons turmeric powder and fry them all together for 3-4 minutes ( or untill the smell of ginger and garlic dissappears).
    Now add the chicken mixture anlong with 2 glasses water and cook the chicken for 20 mins on low flame. After that remove only the chicken pieces and keep aside.



  5. Preparation for Dum

    6. Add half of the rice to the gravy, add chicken pieces, add other half of the rice, layer it with the fried onions.
    Close the kadai and keep some weight on the lid or seal it with dough or wet cloth so the heat doesn't escape. Cook for 5 mins in high flame and then another 10 mins in low flame. Turn of the flame and leave it for another 10 mins. Now open the lid, give it a good mix and

    Enjoy your Chicken Dum Biryani !
